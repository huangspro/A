Bellary 2008

Study characteristics

Methods

Enhanced diabetes care to patients of South Asian ethnic origin (the United Kingdom Asian Diabetes Study): a cluster randomised controlled trial

Cluster RCT (21 clusters), conducted in inner‐city practices in the United Kingdom

Two arms: 1) Control (control arm) and 2) Intervention (intervention arm)

Participants

Control arm N: 618

Intervention arm N: 868

Diabetes type: type 2

Mean age: 57.0 ± 11.9

% Male: 52.0

Longest follow‐up: 24 months

Interventions

Control arm:

1) Clinician education

Intervention arm:

1) Team changes

2) Clinician education

3) Patient education

Outcomes

1) Statins

2) Antihypertensives (any)

3) Systolic blood pressure

4) Diastolic blood pressure
