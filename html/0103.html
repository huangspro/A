Clifford 2002

Study characteristics

Methods

A randomised controlled trial of a pharmaceutical care programme in high‐risk diabetic patients in an outpatient clinic

Patient RCT, conducted in a diabetes clinic in Australia

Two arms: 1) Control (control arm) and 2) PCP ‐ pharmaceutical care programme (intervention arm)

Participants

Control arm N: 25

Intervention arm N: 48

Diabetes type: type 1 and Type 2

Mean age: 60.5 ± 12.0

% Male: 55.0

Longest follow‐up: 6 months

Interventions

Control arm:

None

Intervention arm:

1) Case management

2) Team changes

Outcomes

1) HbA1c, mean % (SD)

Control arm: pre 8.5 (1.6), post 8.1 (1.6)

Intervention arm: pre 8.4 (1.4), post 8.2 (1.5)

Funding source

This study was funded by The Society of Hospital Pharmacists of Australia, Pharmacia and Upjohn Contemporary Therapeutics Research Grant
