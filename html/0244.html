Cagnacci 2009a

Methods

Randomized trial in Italy; enrollment Mar to Jun 2007; randomization following a "computer‐generated list." Sample size estimate based on detecting significant change in glucose test results.

Participants

28 healthy women, 18 to 35 years old. Inclusion criteria: BMI < 25 kg/m2; normal menses, no OC or medicine that could affect glucose or lipid metabolism in past 12 months.

Interventions

Desogestrel 150 µg plus EE 20 µg (N=14) versus chlormadinone acetate 2 mg plus EE 30 µg (N=14); duration 6 months

Outcomes

After 'frequently sampled intravenous glucose tolerance test': AUC for glucose, insulin, and C‐peptide

Notes

No mention of blinding except for the data analysis.
Losses: 14% overall; 2 from each group (2 discontinued for personal reasons, 1 was lost, and 1 withdrew)

Risk of bias

Bias
